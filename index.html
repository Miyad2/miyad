FAQ's

What services do you offer as a web designer and developer?

I offer website design, front-end development, custom WordPress solutions, responsive website development, website maintenance, and performance optimization.

Do you build mobile-friendly websites?

Absolutely! All the websites I create are fully responsive and optimized for seamless user experiences across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Yes, I can give your existing site a modern, user-friendly makeover while improving performance and functionality.

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline depends on the project’s complexity. Simple websites usually take 1-3 weeks, while more complex ones can take longer.

Will I be able to update my website after it’s launched?

Definitely! If you're using WordPress, I can guide you on how to make updates yourself.

Do you offer website maintenance after launch?

Yes, I provide ongoing maintenance services to keep your website up-to-date and running smoothly.

How much does a website cost?

Pricing depends on your specific requirements. Contact me, and I'll provide a custom quote based on your project.

Do you offer SEO services?

While I focus on design and development, I follow best practices for on-page SEO. If you need advanced SEO, I can recommend trusted specialists.
